### Case Study 3: Cyber Protect - South African Healthcare Platform

**Profile:**
- **Location**: Cape Town, South Africa
- **Business**: Patient records management
- **Users**: 15,000 patients
- **Challenge**: HIPAA compliance, cyber threats

**GaiaShield Intervention:**
- **Events Analyzed**: 3 security incidents in 1 day
  1. Phishing email (typosquatting: paypa1-verify.com)
  2. Malicious URL (fake Microsoft login)
  3. Brute-force attack (47 failed login attempts)

- **Actions Taken**:
  - Blocked all 3 events automatically
  - Alerted security team with evidence
  - Implemented IP blacklisting

**Result:**
- **Data Breach Prevented**: 15,000 patient records protected
- **Compliance Maintained**: HIPAA audit passed
- **Financial Impact**: 
  - Avoided $500k in breach costs
  - Avoided $2M in regulatory fines
  - **Total Savings**: $2.5M

**Quote:**
